A Message to the Professor

Out of all the classes I had this semester, this class was definitely one I enjoyed the most. The creative freedom in the assignments, the lack of tedious essay writing and the fact that I got to program again in a class really made this class shine. I gotta thank you too prof, for making it a fun and enjoyable course. I may not have interacted with the class much as a result of my introversion, but I really did like watching the lectures and hearing you ramble on about whatever came to your mind. It was so much more interesting than hearing philosophical analyses or having the strucutal diffential shoved in your face for 2 hours.

Anyways, thanks for the fun semester Professor Lange.

- Osajiele
