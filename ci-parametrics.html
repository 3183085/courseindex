ASSIGNMENT 2: PARAMETRICS AND GENERATION

Assignment Requirements

For this assignment, we were tasked with developing daily "code sketches" of a letter of the alphabet using p5.js. My letter of my choice was "V".
Rather than constructing a single project, our goal was to allot daily time to explore generative art. With our assigned letter as your inspiration, we were to create 7 "code sketches" over the course of 3 weeks, resulting in 21 total.


Learning Objectives

  • Develop their knowledge of fundamental programming concepts.
  • Explore new and non-traditional ways of making beyond the parameters of existing design software.
  • Refine formal approach through experimentation and iteration.
  • Learn how a generative approach can aid in future meaning-making.
  • Embrace happy ‘mistakes’: unexpected results.
  • Develop a daily practice of learning through making.
  • Problem solve alone and with other students.
